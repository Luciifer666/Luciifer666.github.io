Section of the Glassmorphism

Glassmorphism is the latest user interface design principle in vogue. It is a subset of the design principle called Neumorphism or design that mimics the real object in the environment for a more objective basis. Taking this principle, glassmorphism actually incorporates glass effects into the look and feel of the interface.
